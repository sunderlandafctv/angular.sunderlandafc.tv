1950's

Big Money signings and glamorous teams but ultimately no trophies.

A dramatic decade when we became known as the 'Bank of England Club' with big money signings flowing into the club on a regualr basis. Our forward line would contact the legendary Shackleton, Ford, Chilsolm and Bingham. We narrowly missed out on the league title in 1954 and lost in two FA Cup semi finals before being rocked by the illegal payments scandal that would lead to our first ever relegation.
