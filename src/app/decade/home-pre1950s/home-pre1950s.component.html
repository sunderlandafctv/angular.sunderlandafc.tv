Pre 1950's

One of the giants of English football with 6 League titles and 1 FA Cup victory.

The Glory Years. We won the league title 6 times and some of the biggest names in football Campbell, Holley, Buchan, Halliday, Gurney and Carter wore the famours Red and White Shirts. The 1937 FA Cup winning team were possiblt the most exciting team inour illustrious history. At the end of the 1940's Sunderland had accumulated more top flight points than any other team in England and had never been relegated.
