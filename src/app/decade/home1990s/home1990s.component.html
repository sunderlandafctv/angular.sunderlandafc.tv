1990's

A glorious run to the FA Cup final and a new dawn as the Stadium of Light opened.

Malcolm Crosby led us to the FA Cup final in 1992, until mediocriy kicked in. Peter Reid would then build the finest Sunderland team since the 1950's including the legendary Quinn, Phillips, Ball, Johnston and Sorenson as the Stadium of light rocked to some fantastic football.
