1970's

FA Cup triumph and a team of heros amongst a decade spent mainly outside the top flight.

The dramatic FAC Cup in 1973 is one of the highlight of our history and was followed by European football. Great Players like Kerr, Rowell, Robson and Watson graced Roker Park but this was ultimately a decade of 2nd division football.
